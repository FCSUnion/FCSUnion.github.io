About Us

The Edinburgh High School Students Union is a Union of Secondary School Students in Edinburgh. It aims to provide a voice for all students in the city and create a more positive high school experience for all.


Membership is free for anyone currently attending an Edinburgh High School.

Advantages of joining include:

  • Getting your voice heard and put pressure on those who can make changes
  • Collectively raising the issues that matter to you
  • Being part of a network of other students with common interests and goals
  • Helping work towards a better education experience for all
